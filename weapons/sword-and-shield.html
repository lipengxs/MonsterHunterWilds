Sword & Shield

The perfect balance of offense, defense, and unmatched versatility

Weapon Characteristics

A weapon that boasts maneuverability and rapid attacks. You can use the shield both to guard and to attack. You can even use some items while your weapon is drawn.

Item Usage While Drawn

The Sword & Shield is the only weapon that allows you to use items without sheathing. This unique ability makes it incredibly versatile in combat, allowing you to heal, buff, or use tools without losing offensive momentum.

Core Mechanics

Perfect Rush

A powerful combo that requires precise timing for maximum damage. Each successful timing increases the damage of subsequent hits, making it a devastating attack sequence when mastered.

Shield Bash

Use your shield offensively to deal blunt damage and potentially stun monsters. This move also provides excellent mobility and can be used to close distances quickly.

Falling Shield Bash

A unique aerial attack that can mount monsters and deal significant damage. This move provides excellent mobility and can be used to initiate mounting attacks from any position.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Quick Item Usage

Take advantage of your ability to use items while drawn. This allows you to maintain aggressive positioning while still supporting yourself and teammates with items.

Shield Timing

While not as strong as dedicated shields, proper timing with your shield can save you from dangerous attacks. Use it reactively rather than holding it up constantly.

Element & Status

The fast attack speed makes the Sword & Shield excellent for applying elemental damage and status effects. Consider having multiple weapons for different situations.

Perfect Rush Mastery

Practice the timing of Perfect Rush as it's your highest damage potential. The visual and audio cues will help you nail the timing for maximum damage.

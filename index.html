Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

Minimum: Windows 10 64-bit, Intel i5/AMD Ryzen 5, 8GB RAM, NVIDIA GTX 1060/AMD RX 580

When is the release date?

Marvel Rivals is scheduled for release in late 2024. Join our beta test starting April 2024!

Is the game free to play?

Yes! Marvel Rivals will be free to play with optional cosmetic purchases.

How many heroes are available at launch?

The game will launch with over 20 playable heroes and villains from across the Marvel universe, with more being added regularly post-launch.

What game modes are available?

Marvel Rivals features various 6v6 game modes including Team Deathmatch, Objective Control, and special event modes. More modes will be added based on community feedback.

How do I join the beta test?

Sign up on our official website and register for the closed beta. Selected players will receive an email invitation with download instructions.

Will there be cross-platform play?

Cross-platform play is planned for future updates, allowing PC players to team up with console players.

How does the team composition work?

Teams consist of 6 players with different roles (Duelist, Vanguard, Strategist). Strategic hero combinations and team synergies are key to victory.

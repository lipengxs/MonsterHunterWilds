Hammer

The king of KO damage, bringing devastating impact to every hunt

Weapon Characteristics

A blunt weapon with destructive power and good mobility. Charge your attacks to make them more powerful. You can even stun monsters by landing multiple hits to their head.

Charge System

The Hammer's charge system allows you to unleash increasingly powerful attacks based on how long you charge.

Level 1

Quick upswing with good mobility

Level 2

Powerful side swing with wide reach

Level 3

Devastating spinning attack or brutal overhead smash

Core Mechanics

Charge Attacks

Hold the attack button to charge up powerful strikes. Different charge levels offer different attacks, allowing you to adapt to any situation. Moving while charging enables unique follow-up attacks.

Impact Damage

All Hammer attacks deal blunt damage, which is particularly effective at breaking monster parts and dealing KO damage when hitting the head. Multiple successful hits to a monster's head will cause it to be stunned.

Power Charge

Power up your hammer to increase damage and grant flinch resistance. This state enhances all your attacks and changes some of their properties, making your strikes even more devastating.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Head Hunter

Always aim for the monster's head. The Hammer's KO potential is its greatest strength, and landing head hits consistently will give your team valuable openings.

Charge Management

Learn which charge attacks to use in different situations. Level 3 charges can be released into either a spinning attack for mobility or a big bang for maximum damage.

Positioning

Use slopes and ledges to your advantage. The Hammer has excellent aerial attacks that can lead to mounting opportunities and devastating spinning attacks.

Power Charge

Keep your Power Charge active as much as possible. The damage boost and flinch resistance are crucial for maximizing your effectiveness in combat.

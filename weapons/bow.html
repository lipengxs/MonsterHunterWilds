Pro Tips & Strategies

Critical Distance

Maintain optimal range for maximum damage. The reticle color indicates when you're at the right distance for your shot type.

Coating Management

Start with Power Coatings for raw damage, then switch to status coatings when needed. Bring materials to craft more during hunts.

Stamina Control

Monitor your stamina when charging shots and dodging. Use dash juice or constitution skills to improve stamina efficiency.

Shot Type Selection

Choose your shot type based on the monster's size and weakpoints. Rapid for small targets, Spread for large areas, Pierce for long monsters.

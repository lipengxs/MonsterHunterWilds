Team-Up System

What is Team-Up?

Team-Up Abilities are a unique feature in Marvel Rivals that allows heroes to combine their powers, creating devastating combo attacks. When specific heroes work together, they can trigger special synergy effects that dramatically enhance their combat effectiveness.

Key Features

Power Synergy

Heroes with complementary abilities can combine their powers to create unique effects. For example, Hulk can enhance Iron Man's Nano Armor with gamma energy, enabling incredibly powerful attacks.

Strategic Combinations

Choose your team composition wisely - some heroes work better together than others. Consider both offensive and defensive synergies when building your team.

Tactical Advantage

Team-Up abilities can turn the tide of battle. Use them strategically to control areas, deal massive damage, or provide crucial support to your team.

Team-Up Tips

  • Role Balance: Consider combining different hero roles (Damage, Support, Control) for a well-rounded team composition.
  • Timing is Key: Coordinate with your teammates to maximize the impact of Team-Up abilities.
  • Map Awareness: Use Team-Up abilities in conjunction with destructible environments for maximum effect.
  • Communication: Stay in touch with your team to coordinate powerful ability combinations.

Classic Team-Up Examples

Offensive Combo

Hulk + Iron Man's Gamma Unibeam: Combines gamma radiation with advanced technology for devastating area damage.

Control Combo

Luna Snow + Namor's Frozen Tides: Merges ice and water powers for enhanced crowd control.

Support Combo

Adam Warlock + Star-Lord + Mantis: Provides powerful healing and team support capabilities.

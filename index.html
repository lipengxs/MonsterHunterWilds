About Monster Hunter Wilds

Monster Hunter Wilds is the next evolution in the critically acclaimed action RPG series. As a hunter assigned to the Forbidden Lands Research Commission, you'll explore a vast, seamless world where ecosystems live and breathe, and monsters interact in complex food chains. This mysterious region, only recently cleared for exploration by the Guild, holds secrets waiting to be uncovered.

Living Ecosystem

Experience a truly dynamic world where weather patterns, time of day, and monster interactions create an ever-changing hunting ground. Watch as predators hunt prey and herds protect their young in a persistent environment.

Enhanced Combat

Master the new Focus Mode to target specific monster parts, unleash devastating Focus Strikes, and execute perfectly timed Power Clashes. The combat system has evolved while maintaining the strategic depth fans love.

Seamless World

Explore vast, interconnected regions without loading screens. Move freely between the Windward Plains and Scarlet Forest, each with their own apex predators and unique environmental challenges.

How to Play

1. Choose Your Weapon

Select from 14 unique weapon types, each with its own playstyle and mechanics. From the versatile Sword & Shield to the explosive Heavy Bowgun, find your perfect hunting style.

2. Prepare for the Hunt

Craft armor, prepare meals, and select items for your loadout. Every hunt requires careful preparation and strategy.

3. Track Your Prey

Use the new Scout Flies and environmental clues to track monsters across vast landscapes. Learn their behaviors and patterns.

4. Master the Combat

Engage in intense battles using the enhanced combat system. Time your attacks, dodge monster moves, and utilize the environment to your advantage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in Monster Hunter Wilds?

Monster Hunter Wilds introduces revolutionary features including dynamic weather systems, seamless open world exploration, enhanced mounting mechanics with the Seikret companion, and a new Focus Mode combat system. The game also features pack behavior for monsters and a living ecosystem that changes based on weather and time of day.

How does the weather system work?

Each area has three distinct states: Plenty (lush vegetation, stable weather), Fallow (reduced activity), and Inclemency (extreme weather conditions). These states affect monster behavior, available resources, and hunting strategies.

What is the Seikret companion?

The Seikret is a raptor-like mount that aids in exploration and combat. It can carry a second weapon for quick switching during hunts, guide you to objectives, and allow you to heal and gather materials while traveling. It also enables swift traversal and tactical advantages during hunts.

Can I play solo?

Yes! Monster Hunter Wilds is fully playable solo, with AI Support Hunters and Palico companions available to assist you. You can also seamlessly switch between solo and 4-player cooperative multiplayer modes.

Does the game support cross-platform play?

Yes, Monster Hunter Wilds features full cross-platform play between PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC players, allowing hunters to team up regardless of their platform choice.

What accessibility features are available?

The game includes various accessibility options, including an arachnophobia mode that replaces spider-like creatures with alternative models. Additional accessibility features will be detailed closer to launch.

Will there be post-launch content?

Yes, downloadable content is planned, focusing on cosmetic items such as layered armor. Capcom has confirmed there will be no "pay-to-win" items in the game.

How does the armor system work?

Players can now choose between armor designs regardless of their character's gender. Additionally, Set Bonuses can be activated by wearing armor from the same monster type category, offering more flexibility in builds.

About the Insect Glaive

The Insect Glaive is a unique weapon that pairs a versatile polearm with a customizable Kinsect companion. Known for its exceptional aerial mobility and buff management system, it offers a dynamic playstyle that emphasizes both ground and air combat.

Weapon Characteristics

  • Weapon Type: Bladed Staff
  • Combat Style: Technical/Aerial
  • Damage Type: Cutting
  • Special Feature: Kinsect Management

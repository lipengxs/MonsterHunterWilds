Demon & Archdemon Modes

The Dual Blades feature a unique power-up system that allows you to enter enhanced states for increased damage and mobility.

Normal Mode

Basic attacks and mobility

Demon Mode

Enhanced attacks and new moves at the cost of stamina

Archdemon Mode

Maintains some Demon Mode benefits without stamina cost

Core Mechanics

Demon Mode

Enter a powered-up state that enhances your attacks and mobility at the cost of stamina drain. Build up your demon gauge while in this mode to access Archdemon Mode.

Blade Dance

A rapid series of attacks that deals massive damage when all hits connect. This move is especially effective on larger monsters or when targeting specific parts.

Demon Flurry Rush

A quick dash attack that can be used to close distance or reposition while dealing damage. Perfect for maintaining aggressive pressure on monsters.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Stamina Management

Monitor your stamina carefully in Demon Mode. Use dash juice or other stamina items to extend your Demon Mode duration for maximum damage output.

Positioning

Use your superior mobility to stay close to monsters. The Dual Blades' short range means you need to be aggressive with positioning to maintain damage.

Element & Status

Dual Blades excel at applying elemental damage and status effects due to their rapid hit rate. Always bring the appropriate elemental weapons for each monster.

Mode Switching

Learn to switch between modes effectively. Use Demon Mode for burst damage windows, and maintain Archdemon Mode for consistent damage output.

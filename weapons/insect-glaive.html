Core Mechanics

Kinsect Management

Direct your Kinsect to gather extracts from specific monster parts. Different Kinsect types offer various speeds, power levels, and dust effects.

Aerial Mobility

Use vaults and air dashes to maintain altitude and position yourself for attacks. Chain aerial moves to stay airborne and avoid ground-based threats.

Extract Combinations

Combine different extracts for enhanced effects. Triple Up provides the maximum boost to all stats and unlocks powerful combo finishers.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Extract Priority

Always prioritize getting Red extract first, followed by White. Red extract significantly improves your damage output and combo potential.

Aerial Efficiency

Don't rely solely on aerial attacks. Mix ground and air combos for optimal DPS, using aerial moves for positioning and mounting opportunities.

Kinsect Selection

Choose your Kinsect based on your playstyle. Speed-type Kinsects are great for gathering extracts quickly, while Power-types deal more damage.

Buff Management

Keep track of your extract durations. Refresh buffs before they expire, and try to maintain Triple Up as much as possible during combat.

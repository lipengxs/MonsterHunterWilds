Characters

Discover the legendary hunters and remarkable personalities who will shape your journey through the mysterious Forbidden Lands. Each character brings unique abilities, stories, and expertise to your adventure.

Alma

Elite Hunter

A skilled hunter known for her exceptional tracking abilities and mastery of multiple weapon types. Her deep understanding of monster behavior makes her an invaluable mentor to new hunters.

Master Hunter Mentor Strategist

Gemma

Research Scholar

A brilliant researcher specializing in monster ecology and ancient civilizations. Her research has been crucial in understanding the mysteries of the Forbidden Lands and its unique ecosystem.

Scholar Researcher Analyst

Nata

Supply Master

The resourceful supply master who ensures hunters are well-equipped for their missions. Her expertise in resource management and item crafting is essential for the success of every expedition.

Logistics Crafting Management

Athos

Veteran Commander

A seasoned hunter commander who leads the defense of the research base. His tactical expertise and years of experience make him an invaluable leader in critical situations.

Leader Tactician Veteran

Olivia

Quest Manager

The dedicated quest manager who coordinates hunting assignments and expedition planning. Her attention to detail and organizational skills ensure smooth operations at the hunting base.

Coordinator Planner Support

Erik

Master Blacksmith

A legendary blacksmith whose craftsmanship is renowned throughout the hunting world. His ability to forge powerful weapons and armor from monster materials is unmatched.

Craftsman Weaponsmith Innovator

Fabius

Trade Master

The charismatic merchant who manages the trading post. His vast network of contacts and keen business sense ensure a steady flow of rare materials and essential supplies.

Merchant Negotiator Supplier

Werner

Research Director

A distinguished scholar and leader of the Research Commission. His groundbreaking research into monster ecology and ancient civilizations has revolutionized our understanding of the Forbidden Lands.

Director Scholar Visionary

Switch Axe

Morphing Weapon Master

Weapon Overview

The Switch Axe is a transforming weapon that alternates between a heavy axe and an explosive sword mode. Master both forms to unleash devastating combos and powerful phial bursts.

  • Mobility: Medium
  • Defense: Low

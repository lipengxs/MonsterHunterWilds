Pro Tips & Strategies

Ammo Selection

Choose ammo types based on monster weaknesses and size. Pierce ammo excels against large monsters, while spread is effective on smaller targets.

Positioning

Maintain optimal range for your chosen ammo type. Use terrain and obstacles for cover when reloading or entering siege mode.

Siege Mode Timing

Use siege mode when monsters are trapped, stunned, or focused on other targets. Always have an escape route planned.

Special Ammo Usage

Save Wyvern Ammo for wake-up hits on sleeping monsters. Use machine gun mode during extended openings for sustained damage.

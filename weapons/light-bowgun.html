Core Mechanics

Ammo Management

Different ammo types have varying recoil, reload speeds, and clip sizes. Master these characteristics to maintain optimal DPS while staying mobile.

Special Ammo

Wyvernblast mines can be planted on the ground or monsters, detonating when struck for massive damage. Strategic placement is key.

Mobility Techniques

Utilize evade reloads and sliding shots to maintain aggression while repositioning. Stay mobile to avoid attacks and maintain optimal range.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Ammo Selection

Choose your ammo types based on the monster's weaknesses and your bowgun's rapid fire capabilities. Always bring materials to craft more during hunts.

Critical Distance

Maintain optimal range for maximum damage. The reticle color indicates when you're at the right distance for your selected ammo type.

Wyvernblast Placement

Place mines in monster paths or during knockdowns. They can be detonated by any damage source, including teammate attacks.

Mobility Management

Use evade reloads to maintain DPS while repositioning. Sliding shots can help you quickly close or create distance while attacking.

Long Sword

Master the way of the blade with precision, speed, and devastating counters

Weapon Characteristics

A weapon that excels at swift movements and combos. With a sharp eye and good timing, you can counter incoming attacks. Landing certain attacks will raise the level of the Spirit Gauge, empowering your blade.

Spirit Gauge System

The Long Sword's unique Spirit Gauge fills as you land attacks. Use Spirit Blade attacks to level up your gauge through White → Yellow → Red, with each level increasing your damage output.

Core Mechanics

Spirit Combo

Execute a series of Spirit Blade attacks to increase your Spirit Gauge level. The final hit of a successful Spirit Combo will raise your gauge to the next level, enhancing your damage output.

Counter Attacks

The Long Sword features multiple counter moves that can negate monster attacks while delivering powerful strikes. Perfect timing is rewarded with increased Spirit Gauge gain and damage bonuses.

Special Sheathe

Enter a special stance that allows for two powerful follow-up attacks: Iai Slash for quick damage, or Iai Spirit Slash for a powerful counter that can maintain your Spirit Gauge level if timed perfectly.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Spirit Management

Maintain your red Spirit Gauge level by using counters and Iai Spirit Slash. A red gauge significantly increases your damage output, making it crucial for optimal DPS.

Counter Timing

Practice your counter timing against different monster attacks. Perfect counters not only keep you safe but also provide opportunities for massive damage and Spirit Gauge gains.

Positioning

Use the Long Sword's excellent mobility to position yourself near monster weak points. The fade slash is particularly useful for repositioning while maintaining offensive pressure.

Combo Flexibility

Learn to adapt your combos based on the situation. The Long Sword's versatile moveset allows you to seamlessly transition between normal attacks, Spirit Blade combos, and counters.

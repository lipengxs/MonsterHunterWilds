Best Heroes: Meta Tier List (Prelaunch)

A comprehensive tier list based on closed beta performance

S Tier
Venom Luna Snow Hela The Punisher Thor Rocket Raccoon

These are the top characters in the game. They excel in any match and are likely candidates for balance adjustments before launch.

A Tier
Magneto Adam Warlock Doctor Strange Mantis Scarlet Witch

Solid picks that offer good balance between damage and utility. While not quite as dominant as S Tier characters, they are definitely worth mastering.

B Tier
Groot Jeff the Land Shark Iron Man Storm Star-Lord Namor Loki

These characters can be very effective in the right hands but may require more skill and practice to master.

C Tier
Hulk Magik Spider-Man Black Panther Peni Parker

These characters currently struggle to fulfill their intended roles effectively and may need significant buffs or reworks.

Best Heroes for New Players

A beginner's guide to selecting your first Marvel Rivals characters

The Different Role Options

Marvel Rivals features 3 distinct roles:

  • Duelist: Attackers focused on damage output and flanking
  • Vanguard: Tanks that protect objectives and teammates
  • Strategist: Support characters with healing and utility abilities

Best Beginner Duelist: The Punisher

A straightforward attacker with familiar FPS mechanics and strong damage output. Perfect for players new to the genre.

Weapons in Monster Hunter Wilds

Monster Hunter Wilds features 14 unique weapon types, each with its own distinct playstyle, moveset, and mechanics. Whether you prefer quick, agile weapons or heavy-hitting powerhouses, there's a weapon type for every hunter.

The weapons are divided into three main categories:

Melee Weapons

Close-range weapons that excel in direct combat, featuring both light and heavy options.

Technical Weapons

Weapons with complex mechanics that reward mastery and precise timing.

Ranged Weapons

Long-range options that allow hunters to attack from a safe distance.

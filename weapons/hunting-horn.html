Core Mechanics

Melody System

Each attack contributes to your melodies, which can be performed to grant powerful buffs to yourself and allies. Different combinations of attacks create different melodies with unique effects.

Echo Bubbles

Place special bubbles on the battlefield that provide beneficial effects to hunters who pass through them. Strategically place these bubbles to support your team's positioning and combat flow.

Impact Damage

Like the Hammer, the Hunting Horn deals blunt damage and can stun monsters with repeated hits to the head. This makes it both a support and offensive powerhouse.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Buff Management

Keep track of your buff durations and try to maintain key buffs like Attack Up. Refresh buffs before they expire to maintain maximum team effectiveness.

Positioning

Position yourself to hit the monster's head while staying close to teammates. This allows you to deal KO damage while keeping allies within buff range.

Echo Bubble Placement

Place Echo Bubbles in areas where teammates are likely to move through during combat. Consider monster movement patterns when placing bubbles.

Combat Flow

Don't stop attacking to play songs. Your attacks are part of your melody system, so maintain aggressive combat while building up your buffs.

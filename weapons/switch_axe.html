Core Mechanics

Morph Attacks

Switch between axe and sword modes mid-combo for powerful morphing attacks. These transitions deal high damage and can be used to reposition.

Zero Sum Discharge

A powerful finishing move that latches onto monsters and unleashes explosive damage. Time it right to maximize damage and avoid getting thrown off.

Amped State

Build up energy through sword mode attacks to enter an amped state, increasing damage output and unlocking powerful combo finishers.

Weapon Tutorial

Pro Tips & Strategies

Mode Management

Use axe mode for mobility and reaching high parts, then switch to sword mode when you find openings for sustained damage.

Phial Selection

Choose your phial type based on the monster's weaknesses. Power Phial is great for raw damage builds, while Element Phial works well with high element weapons.

Discharge Timing

Save Zero Sum Discharge for when monsters are exhausted or trapped. You can also use it to avoid powerful attacks while dealing damage.

Combo Efficiency

Learn which combos build amp state quickly and practice morphing attacks to maintain aggressive pressure while staying mobile.

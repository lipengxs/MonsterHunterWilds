Core Mechanics

Phial Management

Charge phials through sword attacks, store them with R2+Circle, and unleash them through various axe mode attacks. Overcharged phials will cause your attacks to bounce.

Super/Ultra Burst

Super Amped Element Discharge (SAED) is your most powerful attack, consuming all phials for massive damage. Can be canceled into a regular AED for more controlled bursts.

Guard Points

Special blocking animations that provide increased guard capability and can lead directly into counter-attacks. Master these for optimal defensive play.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Phial Management

Keep your phials charged but avoid overcharging. Use sword mode to build charge safely, and store phials when your shield glows red.

Guard Point Timing

Practice guard point timings in the training area. The morph animation from sword to axe mode is the most commonly used guard point.

SAED Positioning

Position yourself carefully before unleashing SAED, as the attack has a long animation. Aim for the head with Impact Phials for potential KOs.

Shield Charge

Keep your shield charged for increased guard capability and access to SAED. Cancel an SAED into an AED by holding back and Triangle during the windup.

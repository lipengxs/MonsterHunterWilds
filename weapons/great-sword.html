Great Sword

Master the art of timing and positioning with Monster Hunter's mightiest blade

Weapon Characteristics

A weighty weapon with slower movement and attacks, but each blow packs a powerful punch. Suitable for hit-and-run tactics or even guarding, the Great Sword is highly adaptable.

Core Mechanics

Charged Attacks

Hold down the attack button to charge your strikes. Each level of charge increases damage significantly, with the True Charged Slash being the most powerful single hit in the game.

Tackle

Use the tackle move to power through monster attacks while charging, gaining hyper armor and positioning yourself for powerful counterattacks.

Guard

While not as effective as dedicated shield weapons, the Great Sword can block attacks in a pinch, making it a versatile option for both offense and defense.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Positioning is Key

Always try to position yourself where the monster will be, not where it is. The Great Sword's charges require prediction and forethought.

Master the Tackle

The tackle is your best friend. Use it to power through attacks and maintain your charge level for devastating counters.

Know When to Sheathe

The Great Sword's mobility is poor when drawn. Sheathe your weapon to reposition quickly and set up your next big hit.

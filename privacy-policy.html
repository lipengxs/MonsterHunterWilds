Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Monster Hunter Wilds. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect:

  • Website Data: Information about your browsing behavior, preferences, and interactions
  • Account Information: Your email and contact information if you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, and browser details
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, and content preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and enhance your Monster Hunter Wilds website experience
  • Save your preferences for future visits
  • Improve our website content and features
  • Send newsletters and updates about Monster Hunter Wilds
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously and:

  • Use industry-standard encryption for data protection
  • Implement strict access controls
  • Regularly update our security measures
  • Never share your personal information with third parties without consent

Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide relevant content

Policy Updates

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be communicated through website notifications. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • Email: support@monsterhunterwilds.org
  • Website: monsterhunterwilds.org
  • Contact Form: Available on our website
